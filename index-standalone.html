Our Story

A small workshop, a lot of thread.

Silk Élegance started on a kitchen table with a few spools of silk thread and a stack of plain bangles nobody wanted. Today every piece is still wound by hand, one colour at a time, in batches small enough that we notice when a shade runs out.

We work directly with thread artisans, keep our palettes seasonal, and make sure every bangle that leaves our workshop could survive a wedding, a wardrobe change, and a few years of daily wear.
